About StarLight Roleplay

StarLight Roleplay is a premium SAMP server dedicated to providing the most authentic and immersive roleplay experience in Los Santos. Our community has been growing since our launch, bringing together passionate roleplayers from around the world.

With custom scripts, professional staff, and a commitment to quality roleplay, we've created an environment where every player can live their virtual life to the fullest. From law enforcement to criminal organizations, from business owners to everyday citizens - everyone has a place in our city.
